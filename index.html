This map shows cities with more than 5,000,000 people (in 2025).

Population growth between 2024 and 2025 is visualized using green and red colors.

About the measurement

The largest cities in the world, ranked by population, are typically measured by their metropolitan area, which includes the city itself and surrounding areas that are economically and socially integrated.

Source of data:

Largest Cities by Population 2025. (2025-07-12). World Population Review. https://worldpopulationreview.com/cities
